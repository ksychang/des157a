Every Picture Project Development

My concept for the Every Picture Project is called Spaces of Transition. My collection of images includes places where we experience transitions in life, space, time, feelings, relationships, and more.

After completing my interaction experiments, I realized I want to continue incorporating interactions on my page that represent or emphasize transitions depicted in the photos. I have brainstormed some ideas for different sections throughough my page that the user can walk through, and potential interactions I may create for each of them.

To record my ideas, I created the mind map shown on the left. I plan to draw some of my inspiration for the visual design system from other meditation or prayer apps. I will also conduct further experiments on interactions as I nail down the flow of the narrative. My main goal for this project is to practice taking users on a journey and creating a reflective, meaningful digital experience.
